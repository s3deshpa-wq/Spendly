Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the service:

  • Your email address and password (stored as a secure hash) when you register.
  • Expense records you create, including amount, category, date, and any description you provide.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly enter.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate Spendly for you:

  • To authenticate your account and keep your session secure.
  • To display your expense history, summaries, and category breakdowns.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a local database on the server running this application. Passwords are never stored in plain text. We take reasonable precautions to protect your data, but as a personal-use tool, Spendly does not guarantee enterprise-grade security. Do not store sensitive financial credentials within the app.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your data with third parties. The app uses Google Fonts for typography, which may result in your browser making a request to Google's servers when loading a page. No personal or financial data is transmitted in this request.

5. Your Rights

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time. Once deleted, your expense records are permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via the contact information provided in the app.
